use pefile for binary dependency tracking on win32
--------------------------------------------------
(http://dkbza.org/pefile.html)
example usage::

  In [6]: pe=pefile.PE("ppthtml.exe", True)

  In [7]: list(pe.DIRECTORY_ENTRY_IMPORT)
  Out[7]:
  [<pefile.ImportDescData instance at 0x2adb0a6e6a28>,
   <pefile.ImportDescData instance at 0x2adb0a6f1a70>]

  In [8]: [x.dll for x in list(pe.DIRECTORY_ENTRY_IMPORT)]
  Out[8]: ['KERNEL32.dll', 'msvcrt.dll']
